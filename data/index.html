Battery life

  • The laser stations without antenna and display last for more than 24h of use.
  • The laser stations with antenna and display last for roughly 10 hours.
  • The battery life of the display station depends on the display brightness. If the brightness is set to 100% the battery will be empty after 1h30. 5% brightness will give closer to 5h of operation.
